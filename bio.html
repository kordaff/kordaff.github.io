I went on a couple almanac sites, looking for the weather the day I was born. Low of 53.1F, High of 63F

No rain that entire month, usually there's just over an inch of rain in October in Billings MT, falling on seven of the thirty-one days.

I recall from previous searches that Last Train To Clarksville was the number one song that week. Useless trivia hmm?

My mom left my biological-father when I was 3yr old. We moved back to Billings from Helena MT the winter of 1969/70 during one of the worst recorded blizards in Montana history. I do recall that the back seat of the car mom drove was very very cold. One grandpa driving a pickup ahead of us, the other behind us, both loaded with everything we owned. A family heirloom rocking chair fell off but we couldn't stop because the snow was coming down so hard and so fast, we might not have gotten going again.
