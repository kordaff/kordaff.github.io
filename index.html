cms3.perl-user.com is where my test site for cms3.pm is at now. I should have been testing on a secure (read: paid for domain not a free domain from the start) With free domains, you get what you pay for. The version I committed on the 15th has working logins (/api/login) and sessions. /api/show_my_cookies shows the useruuid and sessionuuid cookies that have been set. /api/delete_my_cookies removes them or /api/logout clears the session cookie. Not sure at this point that the useruuid really serves any great utility. I set the name as a global variable named $whoami when cms3 checks the session (each page load).
